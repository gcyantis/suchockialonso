Sunday, August 5: Répondez s'il vous plaît

Please RSVP by this day (including your flight information) so that we can confirm bookings for rooms, travel, food, and events. Rooms in the Chateau will be given first come first serve, so please RSVP soon!

Wednesday, October 17: Arriver

Please arrive in Angers, France by 3:30 pm. From CDG airport, the fastest route is the TGV train (check the tab “TRAVEL”). Once you arrive in Angers, if you do not want to leave the station, there are 2 lounge areas, a supermarket, restaurant, and a piano to play. There is even the “Distributeur D’histoires Courtes”, located in the main lobby facing the welcome bubble, which offers free short stories so you can brush up on your French! If you would like to leave the station, there are endless points of interest to explore.

  • 4PM Transportation from Angers TGV station to Château de Challain-la-Potherie

    • A chartered bus will leave the Angers train station and head to Château de Challain-la-Potherie at 4 pm. The ride is approximately 45 minutes northwest through the Loire Valley. If you are renting a car, please arrive at the château around 5 pm.

  • 7 PM Cuban Pig Roast

    • This event is scheduled to take place outdoors. Please be prepared for cool weather. Traditional Cuban attire is acceptable.

Thursday, October 18: Se gâter

  • 11 AM Chateau Activities

    • This is your chance to relax and explore! The château offers over 80,000 square feet of architectural delight, while the property encompass 72 acres of manicured gardens, mature woods, and a bridged lake. Activities offered include massage, a cooking class, and horseback riding. Please sign-up on the “RSVP” tab.

  • 6 PM A Masked Affair

    • Black tie is requested for this evening event. A mask is required.

Friday, October 19: Explorer

  • 11 AM Brunch

  • 12 PM Winery Excursion

    • The bus will depart from the château at 12 pm for a 45 drive to an exceptional winery southwest of Angers. After a tour of the vineyard, the château will host us for a tasting of their Savennières wines paired with local cheese. If you are driving separately, please arrive at the winery before 1 pm.

  • 4 PM Evening in Angers

    • After the winery excursion, the bus will take us to Angers, where we are free to explore. Dinner will be on your own. The bus will pick us up at the drop-off location at 10:30 pm.
